Server Rules

1. Respect & Civility

Treat all players with respect. No harassment, hate speech, or discrimination of any kind is tolerated.

2. No Cheating

Use of hacks, mods, or exploits is strictly prohibited. Fair gameplay is mandatory for all players.

3. No Griefing

Destroying or damaging other players' properties is forbidden. Respect boundaries and protect builds.

4. Language Policy

Keep chat clean and family-friendly. Avoid profanity and inappropriate language at all times.

5. No Spam

Don't flood the chat with repetitive messages or excessive capitals. Keep communication clear and concise.

6. Staff Authority

Respect all staff decisions. If you disagree, use proper appeal procedures rather than arguing in-game.
